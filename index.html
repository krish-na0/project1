No two rooms are the same here at The Cruze. Some are hot pink, others are teal. Some have hand-stitched camel leather floors, others have hand-mixed lime plaster walls. But they all share our signature style: a blend of mid-century European furniture with the iconic elements of Moroccan design including hand-carved plasterwork & cedar wood ceilings.
