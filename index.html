Welcome to the CRUZE

A Premium Bontique Hotel

At THE CRUZE (a premium bontique hotel) , our signature wellness programs empower you to maintain your routine, no matter how far from you travel . From our renowned Heavenly® Bed, to our on demand Westin LIVING options, we're here to help you be your best on the road. An authentic bontique hotel, far away from the hustle and bustle, surrounded by archaic nature, and blessed with iconic architecture. Discover our discrete hideaway for individualists and epicureans. Discover a class of its own.

The concept and service of the best luxury hotels in Jamshedpur in our sophisticated Executive room and Royal Suite rooms, with the possibility of enjoying a furnished terrace in our Executive Suite King Room and Royal Suite King Room.

Why miss out on something when you can have it all? The surprise is in the mix and with the surprise comes enjoyment. Discover our rooms, our restaurant; the hustle and bustle of joyful Jamshedpur. Enjoy a hotel in the Jamshedpur not far from the station and feel just a few steps away from the sonari airport. Everything accompanied by the most urban and cosmopolitan spirit. Travel, discover, share… Explore our land and feel part of it.

With a plethora of skin, hair & body treatments – Ours Spa is a sanctuary for relaxation. With two elegantly designed outlets, separate for men and women, Aina offers you the luxury of stepping down from your rooms to get all your salon or spa needs taken care of.

Bar

With its enveloping ambiance and eclectic music,The Bar is the perfect choice in the city to indulge, kick back and relax. The bar menu also offer an excellent choice of light and heavy snacks to compliment its extensive and choice selection of exotic cocktails, mocktails, imported beer and fine liquor.

--Luxury with authenticity--

No two rooms are the same here at The Cruze. Some are hot pink, others are teal. Some have hand-stitched camel leather floors, others have hand-mixed lime plaster walls. But they all share our signature style: a blend of mid-century European furniture with the iconic elements of Moroccan design including hand-carved plasterwork & cedar wood ceilings. Also on our list are oversized bathtubs, soft cotton sheets and the finest mattresses.
